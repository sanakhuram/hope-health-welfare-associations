We are working and dreaming of empowering women with skills and dignity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower underprivileged women and girls from Okara and its adjacent villages in Pakistan. We are committed to equipping them with the requisite tools and skills essential for forging a more promising future for themselves and their families. At our institute, we offer an extensive array of vocational training courses meticulously designed to cater to their distinct needs and aspirations. Our unwavering dedication lies in endowing women with skills and dignity, and we earnestly seek your support to perpetuate this crucial endeavor.

Our Courses and Training Programs:

Basic Computer Skills

Equipping women with fundamental computer training, modern job roles, and entrepreneurial ventures.

Islamic Education

Our Islamic education program focuses on character building and spiritual growth. We provide teaching in Islamic values, ethics, and practices, fostering a strong moral foundation and understanding.

Cooking & Culinary Arts

From basic meal preparation to advanced culinary techniques, our cooking and culinary arts course provides essential skills. This fosters both domestic competence and professional opportunities.

Embroidery & Embellishment Work

Mastering traditional embroidery techniques, transforming simple fabrics into artistic works of art through embellishment.

Stitching

Teaching the art of stitching and tailoring, enabling women to create beautiful garments, either for personal use or to start their own businesses.

Self Grooming Courses

Gain expertise in beauty treatments, haircare, and personal grooming, leading to various careers in the beauty industry.

Our Future Goals:

  • Self-Reliance: Empowering women to become financially independent and capable of supporting their families.
  • Nursing School: Ensuring that all students have access to essential educational resources and a conducive learning environment.
  • Basic School Facilities: Fostering a supportive community environment where women can share experiences, gain confidence, and encourage each other.
  • Transport: Offering transportation services to ensure students can attend school regularly and safely.
  • Empowering Students with Disabilities: Creating an inclusive educational environment and providing specialized support to empower students with disabilities.

STITCH BOX

This shop offers beautiful garments made by graduates of the Stitch Box Training Program. Stitch Box is a non-profit initiative providing free stitching training to empower women. Purchases support the training program and children's education. All profits fund these efforts.
