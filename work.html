  • - Context and application of Hadith in daily life.

Embroidery & Embellishment Work

1-Year Course

  • Silk Work: Techniques for creating intricate designs using silk threads on fabric, including silk shading and thread painting to add depth and realism to embroidery.
  • Ribbon: Crafting decorative patterns and designs using various types and colors of ribbon. Learn ribbon embroidery techniques like ribbon roses, leaves, and intricate floral arrangements.
  • Sequins: Attaching sequins to fabric to create shimmering, decorative patterns. This includes learning how to sew sequins in various patterns, such as lines, clusters, and scattered arrangements.
  • Pearls: Using pearls to embellish garments and accessories, adding an elegant touch. Techniques include sewing pearls in clusters, lines, and creating pearl-centered flowers.
  • Nakshi and Dabka: Traditional embroidery techniques involving coiled wire and beads to create ornate designs. Learn how to handle delicate materials and create patterns that include flowers, paisleys, and geometric designs.
  • Zardozi: A form of metal embroidery that involves weaving metallic threads into fabric to create elaborate patterns. Training includes using zari threads, creating motifs, and integrating beads and sequins.
  • Mirror Work: Embellishing fabric with small mirrors to add sparkle and dimension to designs. Techniques include stitching mirrors securely using various methods like shisha stitch, and integrating mirrors into larger designs.
  • Beadwork: Techniques for sewing beads onto fabric to create detailed and textured designs. Includes learning different stitches such as backstitch, couching, and bead weaving for creating patterns and accents.

Additional Support and Facilities

Provision of Essential Materials for Vocational Training:

We supply all requisite materials for our vocational training programs, ensuring that students have the necessary resources to learn and practice their skills effectively.

Uniforms for Students:

To promote equality and professionalism, we provide uniforms for our students, fostering a sense of belonging and pride.

Monthly Stipends for Students:

We provide a reasonable monthly stipend to our students to support their educational journey and alleviate financial burdens.

Facilitation of Marriages for Disadvantaged Girls and Provision of Dowry:

We facilitate marriages for disadvantaged girls and provide them with dowries, supporting them in beginning their new lives with dignity and respect.

Complimentary Medicines and Consultations in OPD:

Our on-site medical facilities offer complimentary medicines and consultations, ensuring the health and well-being of our students.
